Article 4 : Boundaries of natural water  
 
Natural water boundaries are delimited as 
follows:  
 
1º for 
streams, 
rivers 
and 
lakes, 
boundaries are delimited by the line 
reached by the highest waters before 
overflowing;  
 
2º for wetlands, boundaries are delimited 
by a line reached by the highest 
waters in normal circumstances. 
 
 
This line referred to under items 1° and 2° 
constitutes 
the 
starting 
point 
for
